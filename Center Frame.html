About College

Nadimpalli Satyanarayana Raju Institute of Technology (NSRIT) is a dream place for every engineering aspirant. A place with great values, high punctuality, great disciplines, best faculties in every department for efficient teaching - learning process & finally great labs for practical learning.

Many Students have been awarded with great rewards. One of them is G. Sarath Chandra who have been awarded with the University Gold Medal for the academic year 2014-18.

NSRIT is place where you will be able to see many variations of cultures. Academic activities will be seen in parallel to the cultural activities. Many culturals such as Sankranti Sambaralu, Freshers' Day, Farewell day many more will be seen.
